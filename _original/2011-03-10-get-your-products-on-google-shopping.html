Are your products listed on Google Shopping? If not, why not? Does your existing Google Merchant / Froogle / Base feed meet the new required format?

Getting your products listed under Google Shopping is easy to do and bring buying customers straight to your products with the great side-effect is that your products can appear on the 1st page of Search Results bypassing your competitors!

The great news continues - many price comparison web-sites use Google Shopping for their price information so your products start to appear around the web strengthening your chances of getting more customers.

Applications such as 'Red Laser' for iPhone make it easy for mobile customers to find your products simply by scanning bar codes with the phone camera while they're out shopping. (this requires barcodes/UPC/EAN/ISBN data for your products)
