Example Services:

  • A basic deployment package could look like the following:
    • Initial technology assessment
    • Basic account and user setup
    • Setup DNS settings and MX records to get email flowing through Google Apps
    • Email dual delivery setup, to allow you clients to have a smooth transition from old email system to Google Apps
    • Email data migration from old system to new system
    • Training your administrator on managing the Google Apps Account
