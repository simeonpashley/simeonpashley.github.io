Search Engine Optimisation - SEO

Needles by John James

We are engaged in extensive SEO work for Sewing Needle manufacturer Needles by John James as part of an ongoing SEO initiative.
SEO work includes:

  • Keyword Optimisation - identified and maximised the appearance of key phrases that shoppers are looking for and relate to the business, bringing in more visitors.
  • Site Customisation - taking their existing website and making it Search Engine and Visitor friendly by making changes to the existing code.
  • Google Products - exposed their entire product line to Google Product search results resulting in many more visitors.
  • Product SEO - the Product details were improved to maximise the Search Engine Results positions and also to enable them to appear in Google Products.
  • Pay Per Click - we ran a multi-faceted highly optimised Pay Per Click advertising campaign, resulting in more buying visitors at the lowest price possible - typically less than £1 per day.
  • Analytics - we integrated Google Analytics and help the business understand how to use those business metrics. We also advice on what changes are required in order to improve conversion rates and increase sales.
  • SEO Advice - we monitor the website Analytics and advice on broader changes required in order to maximise conversions.

Success so far for John James

Within days of work starting products were appearing in comparison shopping results and generating more business.
As a direct result of our work there was an almost instant 250% improvement in visitor numbers and the bounce rate also dropped resulting in significantly more sales.
At the time of writing, the general trend is a 10% improvement in visitors every week.

  • Site Structure SEO - We made changes to the Site Structure in order to generate more visitors from relevant key phrases.
  • Social Media Feeds - their latest news blog is now automatically syndicated to their Twitter account numeroaccount to engage a wider audience.

Success for Numero

Our key aim was to bring Numero right to the top of the search results for their key phrases and with our help their position is improving on a daily basis.
